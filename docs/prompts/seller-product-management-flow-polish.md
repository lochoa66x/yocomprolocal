# Prompt: Seller Product Management Flow Polish

Polish the seller product management flow.

Improve the private seller dashboard and product edit flow so a non-technical seller clearly understands how to update, publish, hide, share, or delete a product.

Focus on:

- clearer success and error messages after editing, publishing, hiding, or deleting
- stronger distinction between public buyer-facing product pages and private seller controls
- obvious copy/share actions for product links
- safer delete confirmation language
- friendly Spanish-first microcopy
- mobile-first layout

Do not change schema, auth, upload logic, or public product URLs.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Seller dashboard explains whether each product is public or private.
- Published product links are labeled as public links.
- Draft products clearly say they are private.
- Delete controls explain that deletion is permanent.
- Edit page recommends using Borrador when the seller only wants to hide a product.
- Existing auth, upload, schema, and public URL behavior remain unchanged.
